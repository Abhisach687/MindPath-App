Privacy model

No account. No cloud. No trackers.

MindPath uses localStorage for persistent records and sessionStorage for unfinished drafts and today’s temporary recommendation state.

  • No text is sent to an AI service.
  • NLP uses transparent word and phrase scoring in your browser.
  • Clearing browser site data can erase entries unless you export a backup.
!
Safety comes first

This app cannot provide crisis care.

If you may harm yourself or someone else, cannot stay safe, or are in immediate danger, stop using the exercise tools and contact your local emergency service or go to the nearest emergency department now. Tell a trusted person and avoid being alone.

For persistent, severe, or worsening symptoms, contact a licensed mental-health professional or medical clinician. Screening and app recommendations are not a diagnosis.
